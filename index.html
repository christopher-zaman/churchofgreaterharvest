We Believe

The Church of Greater Harvest Ministries has been called to believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God (2 Timothy 3:15-16).There is only one true God who is Lord overall (Deuteronomy 6:44); embodied in one. His son Jesus Christ (John 1:1-4).

We welcome you, to be a part of our mission as we commit our hands to building and restoring family structure in our community.

Come where the soil is rich and the blessings are plentiful

We Believe that Jesus Christ, the Savior for mankind, conceived by the Holy Ghost, born of the Virgin Mary, (Luke 1:30-35, John 1:18;Matthew 1:18-25).

We believe baptism, a total immersion in water (Mark 1:9-10), in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ (Acts 2:38,10:47-48, 19:4-5).

We believe the baptism of the Holy Spirit as the infallible Word of God and is edified be the speaking of another tongue (Acts 2:1-4, 38-39;8:12-17; 10:44-48;19:1-6;1Corithians 12:13).

We believe there are diversities of gifts of the Spirit, but through one Spirit (1 Corinthians 12:14; Acts 5:12-16).

~ Pastor Benjamin

About us

The Church of Greater Harvest Ministries, a 501(c)(3)Non-Profit Organization, and Non-denominational Church was founded upon the calling of Benjamin E. Lee to Pastor in 2006. Pastor Benjamin is the husband of 25+ years to one wife, Charis F. Cornelius-Lee, and 5 blessed children: Kaymon, Chloe, Caleb, Noble, and Malachi.

Our mission is clear, to be a lighthouse dedicated to cultivating the youth, equipping disciples, and connecting families to the lifeline of God's purpose, committing our hands to building family structure in our community for the furtherance of the gospel of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

We are a small group of prayerful loving individuals that welcome all to come out to fellowship and praise the Lord with us.

Autobiography

Pastor Benjamin Lee is the husband of Charis F. Cornelius-Lee and is the proud father of Kaymon, Chloe, Caleb, Noble, and Malachi. In 2006 he founded The Church of Greater Harvest Ministries in Las Vegas, Nevada, a growing non-denominational, multicultural, and multiracial church. He has a passion for studying, preaching, and teaching the word of God. He serves the community humbly, and offers spiritual guidance, officiates wedding ceremonies, and provides consultations, and assistance to grieving families. Pastor Benjamin's mission is building families, equipping disciples, and connecting the lost to the lifeline of God's purpose.

Outreach

The Lord opened the door for The Church of Greater Harvest Ministries to partner with a local catering establishment. We have been fortunate to go into the community and feed the hungry every Saturday since August 2012. We give all Glory to God for this blessed opportunity as we endeavor to meet the needs of the hungry in our community, reaching out to them in the love of Christ our Savior.
